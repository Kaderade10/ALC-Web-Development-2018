About Crazy Bob

Howdy! My name is Robert but over the years I've gotten the nickname Crazy Bob, I'm not sure why. Ever since I was 2 I've been living on this farm. My favorite animal is my chicken Skunny i'm holding in my arm. My next favorite thing about my farm are my flawless pumpkins, every year they have a competition for the biggest pumpkin and of course I win every year. I grow the best pumpkins in the world and to sell them to you!
